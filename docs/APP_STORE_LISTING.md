## Description

```
Real asteroids are flying past Earth right now — and in Rockimals, every one of
them is a friendly space animal!

Rockimals turns NASA's live asteroid data into a playful space zoo. A tiny
asteroid becomes a Mouse. A giant becomes a Whale. Kids watch real space rocks
drift around Earth on a gentle, glowing radar — and every animal they meet is a
real object in space, right now.

WATCH THE LIVE RADAR
An animated, Earth-centred view of today's asteroid animals. Spin it, zoom it,
tap an animal to say hello. Every distance is shown in a way kids understand:
compared to the Moon.

MEET EVERY ANIMAL
Each animal has its own name, size comparison, and fun facts. How big is it —
more like a rabbit or an elephant? How close will it fly? How fast?

PLAY FOUR MINI-GAMES
• Today's Challenge — rank the animals by power
• Power Duel — pick the mightier animal
• Closer or Farther — a feel for distance
• Animal Match — match the animal to its size

Wrong answers are always met with encouragement — never a buzzer.

COLLECT AND FOLLOW
Earn points, unlock animal badges from Mouse to Whale, and follow favourite
animals to see when they visit again.

REAL SCIENCE, GENTLY TOLD
The real numbers — official NASA designations, distances, and speeds — live in
a "grown-up facts" section behind a parental gate, with links to NASA's own
pages for curious parents and older kids.

MADE FOR KIDS, BUILT FOR TRUST
• No ads. No accounts. No data collection. Nothing scary.
• Works offline with bundled sample data — great for car rides and flights.
• Powered by NASA's public Near-Earth Object data.

Rockimals is an independent app and is not affiliated with or endorsed by NASA.
```
